Community Guidelines

GoBlitz strives to maintain a fun, healthy, and safe community for all our users. In order to preserve our mission, we have developed a set of community guidelines (“Guidelines”), which we take very seriously. We have a zero-tolerance policy, and we have the right, in our sole discretion, to remove any content (“Content” as defined set forth below) and/or suspend or terminate any offending user account if we find that any of the following violations of our Guidelines has occurred:
“Content” includes anything that can be seen, heard and/or referenced in any broadcast or broadcast title, any text or images posted to any Comments section, and any information posted or contained in any profile in connection with GoBlitz.
Please read the following carefully and assess your activity and adherence accordingly:
General
GoBlitz users refer to the registered users of GoBlitz, and their behavior is subject to this Guidelines. The behavior of unregistered users on GoBlitz should also be bound by this Guidelines.
GoBlitz users must act in accordance with all applicable local, national, and international laws on GoBlitz. Any content or activities that violate the law or instigate others to carry out illegal activities are prohibited.
GoBlitz only provides live broadcasting service, and any additional commercial activities derived from other platforms are independent behaviors.
1. No Nudity or Overly Sexualized Content.
GoBlitz expressly forbid our broadcasters from engaging in, or broadcasting any of the following sex-related Content that promotes sexual activity, exploitation and/or assault. Sexually implied content or activities are also prohibited, although additional content may be permitted for educational content or pre-approved, but in each case additional restrictions must be observed. Anything related to minors should be cautious and prohibit any content involving minor eroticism. We reserve the right to report misuse of inappropriate content to relevant law enforcement agencies, including but not limited to:
  • Exposure of any genitalia;
  • Inappropriate or revealing clothing;
  • Sexually suggestive or provocative activity, which includes any sexual gestures, posture, dances, and/or general sexual behavior;
  • Sexually charged language, both on camera and/or in the comments section or in the broadcast title;
  • Offers of sexual services including prostitution, escort services, sexual massages, and/or filmed sexual activity.
2. No Bullying, Harassment, or Hateful Language.
Regardless of whether the target is on GoBlitz, we prohibit the use of GoBlitz for all bullying, harassment and hate speech. We do not allow individuals, communities or organizations that perform such behavior to use our services. The type of this content, including but not limited to the following:
  • Discriminatory or humiliating remarks based on: race or ethnic origin, religion or faith, disability, gender, age, nationality, appearance or physical attributes, or sexual orientation/gender identity;
  • Displaying altered images of an individual meant to degrade that individual, or sharing photos or videos of physical bullying of a person for the purpose of shaming the victim;
  • Sharing/displaying personal info of an individual for the purpose of blackmail or harassment;
  • Profanity and/or vulgarity of any kind.
3. No Use of, or Reference To, Illegal Substances or Criminal Activity.
Content-in connection with the use, mention or display of illicit drugs and/or drug paraphernalia is strictly prohibited. Content highlighting tobacco and/or alcohol consumption by users is also strictly prohibited on GoBlitz. Use good judgment, as we reserve the right to punish the accountable and work with law enforcement when we believe there is a legitimate risk of physical harm or direct threat to public safety, including but not limited to the following:
  • Content in connection with the use, mention or display of illicit drugs and/or drug paraphernalia;
  • Smoking or drinking during a broadcast;
  • Cause physical or financial harm to people, animals, property, or businesses directly or indirectly;
  • Used by or in the presence of a minor during a broadcast.
4. No Violent, Self-Harming, or Self-Mutilating Acts or Threats.
GoBlitz has zero tolerance for attempts or threats to harm or kill others. You are ultimately responsible for your own physical and psychological health and well-being, but as a community, we must be respectful of others who may be disturbed or offended by your Content or message. Therefore, we strictly prohibit the following behaviours, including but not limited to:
  • Advocate or encourage suicide and self-injury;
  • Promote or encourage physical violence, sensational or shocking acts that tend to cause harm or discomfort towards yourself / others / child or animals;
  • Dangerous or distracted driving;
  • Over-focusing on extreme or unreasonable bloody or violent content.
5. No Posting of Private Content.
GoBlitz respects the privacy of others, and we work hard to ensure that the information you share with us is secure. Determinations of unauthorized private Content will be determined on a case-by-case basis by GoBlitz in our sole discretion. Please do the same and refrain from the use of Content which invades another person’s privacy or which divulges their private or confidential information. Examples of private information may include, but not limited to the following:
  • Personal addresses or locations that are considered or treated private, and/or non-public personal phone numbers and email addresses;
  • Social security or other national identity numbers or credit card information;
  • Intimate videos or photos taken or distributed without the subject’s consent;
  • Videos or images that are considered private under applicable laws.
6. No Spam.
GoBlitz prohibits users from making commercial profit through our platform. Please do not use misleading or incorrect information, and please avoid the following behaviors, including but not limited to:
  • Deliberately collect likes, tracking Numbers, free virtual currency, or contact other users for commercial purposes without consent;
  • Advertise any product or service, whether it is legal or illegal, through live content or any user information, fields, etc;
  • Publish or distribute unauthorized ads, duplicate messages, or user reports;
  • Phishing;
  • Spread malware or viruses;
  • Sell or share user accounts;
  • Resell GoBlitz services or features.
In addition, if you receive advertising from other users on GoBlitz community, please do not consume and report to us at support@GoBlitz.tv . It is important to understand that we will not be responsible for any consumer actions you make to other users.
7. No Impersonation.
Users are not allowed to impersonate other individuals (whether famous or not) or other GoBlitz users with the intent to mislead, confuse, or deceive others. When choosing a user name, keep the following in mind:
  • Pretending to be anything or anyone is not allowed under any circumstances;
  • The name you use should be the name your friends call you in everyday life and that would appear on your traditional legal identification (e.g. passport, driver’s license, birth certificate, other government issued identification, etc.);
  • If the name you use in everyday life isn’t listed as your user name, please change it immediately. Note that GoBlitz reserves the right to modify user names at any time if we believe, in our sole discretion, that you are attempting to impersonate another person;
  • User names are available on a first-come, first-served basis, in some cases your username may no longer be available. If you believe your desired username is not available due to an fake account, please report the issue to us immediately and we will investigate.
8. No Violation of Copyrights; DMCA Policy.
GoBlitz respects the intellectual property rights of others, and we expect that our community of users will do the same. If you share content on GoBlitz that is not yours or that you do not have the right to share on GoBlitz, you may have infringed on the intellectual property rights of others. Infringement includes the use of any third-party content in your content, derivative creation, or use of other people's copyright content to perform. Any unauthorized content you share on GoBlitz may be subject to removal by the rights owner from GoBlitz, which violates our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Multiple violations may result in a permanent suspension of your account. If you are a copyright owner or a copyright owner’s agent and believe that any Content infringes upon your copyrights, please report it to us immediately. In order to facilitate our investigation, please submit the following information at the same time:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work(s) claimed to have been infringed;
  • Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing or to be the subject of infringing activity and that is to be removed or access to which is to be disabled and information reasonably sufficient to permit the service provider to locate the material, as follows:
    • Display name (e.g. GoBlitz)

    • Broadcast title
    • Date of broadcast
    • Time of broadcast

    • Information reasonably sufficient to permit the service provider to contact you, such as an address, telephone number, and if available, an email address
